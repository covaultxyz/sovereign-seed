Mission Context

The CMMC Compliance Gap

The Defense Industrial Base (DIB) comprises approximately 80,000 contractors required to achieve CMMC 2.0 compliance. Of these, approximately 50,000 handle Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) and must achieve Level 2 certification — which requires a third-party assessment by a Certified Third-Party Assessor Organization (C3PAO).

As of early 2026, there are fewer than 80 active C3PAOs authorized by the Cyber AB to conduct CMMC Level 2 assessments. The supply of assessment capacity is dramatically insufficient for the demand. Ohio manufacturers in the Youngstown-Akron corridor face a 12–18 month wait for C3PAO assessment slots.

The Sovereign Seed initiative addresses this gap from two directions: (1) training workers to implement CMMC-aligned AI governance before assessment, and (2) establishing formal partnerships with Ohio C3PAOs who will benefit from pre-trained, pre-documented assessment candidates.

Architecture

Sovereign Hub Architecture

Three-node governance architecture designed for CMMC Level 2 compliance from the ground up.

Node 01 — BRAIN

Primary Compute Node

Air-gapped primary inference node. Processes all AI tasks. Outputs logged before delivery. No external network connectivity during inference operations.

  • Ryzen 9 7950X, Dual RTX 4090, 128GB ECC
  • 8TB NVMe RAID-5, AES-256 encrypted
  • Physical isolation: locked rack, keycard
  • SIEM integration for AU-3 control family
Node 04 — WITNESS-CACHE

Adversarial Probe + Real-Time Monitor

Dedicated to running adversarial probe library against all Brain Node outputs before they reach the Governor. Operates independently — cannot be influenced by Brain Node outputs.

  • Ryzen 5 7600X, RTX 3060, 32GB DDR5
  • Probe library: 47 structured adversarial inputs
  • Real-time Coherence Score computation
  • Hardware-enforced network isolation from Brain Node
Node 05 — LIBRARIAN

Append-Only Audit Log

Immutable audit log of every Intent Manifest, AI output, Coherence Score, probe result, and Governor decision. The Librarian is the evidence base for C3PAO assessment.

  • Ryzen 5 7600X, RTX 3060, 32GB DDR5
  • 1TB NVMe + 4TB HDD for long-term logs
  • Append-only filesystem: WORM-compliant
  • Satisfies AU-3 (Audit Record Content) controls

Concurrence

C3PAO Technical Concurrence Statement

TECHNICAL CONCURRENCE — CMMC 2.0 LEVEL 2

The undersigned C3PAO technical reviewer has evaluated the Sovereign Hub Architecture as described in Technical Appendix B and the accompanying Concurrence Memo, and finds that the architecture's approach to audit logging, non-repudiation, and boundary enforcement is technically sound and directionally aligned with NIST SP 800-171 Rev 3 requirements for the AU-3 control family.

The Coherence Score methodology, when applied as described with the ≥0.85 production threshold, provides a measurable and auditable governance mechanism that addresses the emerging category of AI-specific controls that CMMC assessors are increasingly encountering in defense manufacturing environments.

Note: This concurrence addresses architectural design only. Full C3PAO assessment requires physical inspection of deployed hardware, review of actual configuration documentation, and evaluation of implemented controls against the full CMMC 2.0 Level 2 control set. See concurrence-memo.html for complete limitations statement.
