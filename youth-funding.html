Recruitment Map

Youth Recruitment Map

Where the youth participants come from. Four pipelines, coordinated across partner organizations.

■

YSU Engineering Students

Undergraduate engineering and technology students recruited through YSU College of STEM and Career Services. Target: 10 per cohort. Pathway: Sovereign Apprentice Program → internship at IGC manufacturer → full-time hire. YSU provides academic credit for Phase 1 and 2.

▲

YouthBuild Partners

Disconnected youth (ages 16–24, not in school, not employed) from Youngstown YouthBuild program. Target: 5–8 per cohort. Pathway: Phase 0 Pre-Apprenticeship (through YouthBuild) → Phase 1 Core Intensive → employer placement. WIOA Youth OJT funding covers wages during Phase 1–2.

◆

CTE High School Partners

Ages 16–18 from Youngstown City Schools CTE manufacturing programs. Target: 5 per cohort. Pathway: HSTI internship at Sovereign Hub → Phase 0–1 as part of CTE sequence → DOL Registered Apprenticeship enrollment at 18. Perkins V funds CTE delivery costs.

▫

Manufacturer Referrals

Children, younger siblings, and community connections of IGC partner manufacturers. Ages 18–24. Target: 3–5 per cohort. These are the warm leads — manufacturers who sign LOIs often have specific young people in mind. IGC members become the primary recruitment channel over time.
