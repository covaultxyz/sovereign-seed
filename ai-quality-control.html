Core Framework

The AI QC Loop

A closed feedback system that validates AI outputs against manufacturing ground truth. The loop is what separates hallucination detection from hallucination tolerance.

◆

Input Layer — Intent Manifest

Every AI task begins with a human-authored Intent Manifest: the expected output, acceptable variance bounds, and failure criteria. The Manifest is the contract that makes auditing possible.

⚠

Witness Node — Adversarial Probe

The Witness Node executes systematic adversarial prompts designed to surface drift, fabrication, and boundary violations. Output logged to the Librarian with timestamps and Coherence Score.

★

Coherence Score — Ground Truth Gate

Score = (Factual Accuracy × 0.4) + (Boundary Compliance × 0.3) + (Intent Alignment × 0.3). Threshold: ≥0.85 for production deployment. Below 0.70 triggers human review.

⚙

Librarian Node — Audit Memory

Every probe, score, and correction is stored in the Librarian's append-only log. The log is the evidence base for C3PAO validation and NSF reporting requirements.
