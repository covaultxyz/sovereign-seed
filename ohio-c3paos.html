Context

Why C3PAO Partnerships Matter

There are fewer than 80 active C3PAOs in the United States authorized to conduct CMMC Level 2 assessments. Against a demand base of 50,000+ Ohio DIB contractors who will eventually need Level 2 certification, this creates a structural scarcity that is actually a strategic opportunity for Sovereign Seed.

C3PAOs want pre-trained, pre-documented assessment candidates. An organization that arrives at the assessment with a functioning Librarian Node (AU-3 controls satisfied), a Witness Node (non-repudiation documented), and a trained Governor workforce reduces the C3PAO's assessment time by an estimated 40–60%. That's revenue per hour that C3PAOs care about deeply.

The offer to Ohio C3PAOs is simple: provide a Technical Concurrence Memo for our NSF proposal, and we refer every one of our IGC partner manufacturers to you for their CMMC assessments. For a C3PAO, 3–5 pre-qualified manufacturing clients is worth 6–10 hours of concurrence review time.
