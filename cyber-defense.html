Affordable
Cyber Defense

Four audio recordings covering affordable cyber defense for small manufacturers, the 21-Day Sovereign AI Blueprint, securing Rust Belt factories against AI hallucinations, and training factory workers on AI precision.

Audio Library

Four Recordings

These recordings provide an accessible audio introduction to the Sovereign Seed initiative's core concepts. Use for stakeholder briefings, team orientation, or grant reviewer outreach.

🎧
Recording 01

Affordable Cyber Defense for Small Manufacturers

How small and mid-size manufacturing companies can achieve meaningful cyber defense posture without enterprise budgets. Covers CMMC 2.0 basics, the Sovereign Compute approach, and the three-node governance architecture as an affordable alternative to cloud-based security services.

🎧
Recording 02

Oak Hill's 21-Day Sovereign AI Blueprint

A complete walkthrough of the 21-day execution timeline from March 15 to April 3. Covers the three operator roles (Duane, Hans, You), the four critical path milestones, and the contingency playbook if any milestone slips. Designed for team briefing and stakeholder orientation.

🎧
Recording 03

Securing Rust Belt Factories Against AI Hallucinations

A practitioner-focused deep dive into the specific hallucination risks facing Youngstown-Akron manufacturing operations. Case studies: CNC parameter drift, certification fabrication, scope expansion. The Hallucination Audit Protocol as the specific countermeasure for each failure mode.

🎧
Recording 04

Training Factory Workers on AI Precision

An introduction to the Hallucination Audit Protocol curriculum from the worker's perspective. What does a factory worker actually do in Week 1? Week 4? What does "Strategic Governor" feel like from inside the role? Designed for use in worker recruitment and community outreach.

Usage Guide

How to Use These Recordings

♥

For Funder Outreach

Share Recording 01 (Affordable Cyber Defense) with NSF program officers as supplemental context. Share Recording 02 (21-Day Blueprint) with McGovern Foundation contacts as evidence of operational seriousness.

⚘

For Employer Recruitment

Share Recording 03 (Rust Belt Hallucinations) with Hans Network manufacturers to prime them for the LOI conversation. The specific case studies are designed to trigger recognition: "that happened to us."

✍

For Worker Recruitment

Share Recording 04 (Training Factory Workers) with the YSU continuing education office and IGC partner HR departments. This is the "what is it actually like" recording that makes workers want to apply.
